LMS Part, Stock and Assembly Management System

This system is meant to help small teams organize their electronic components. Create parts and assemblies, print bill of materials, import or export excel files and manage your stock. You may also use the internal time tracking mechanism to keep track on what your time was spent on.

Bold items are currently implemented

Part Management

  • Create, import or export packages. Packages may have multiple names.
  • Create, import or export parts that use formerly defined packages.
  • Add ordering information to parts
  • Search for parts
  • Check what systems/assemblies a part is used in

Assembly Management

  • or import assemblies. These are collections of parts that specify which and how many parts are used to build a system.
  • Search for and view assemblies
  • Import/Export bill of materials
  • Export a list of parts that are needed for a given number of systems to be built. If used with the Stock Manager, the list will contain the number of parts available and the number of parts that are missing

Stock Management

  • Keep track of your stock: How many parts are available and where are they stored?
  • Move parts, combine batches of parts or split parts into multiple batches

Time Tracking

  • Track your time at work
  • Create projects and tasks
  • Start and stop time logging on a specigied task with a few clicks
  • Get an overview of time spend on different tasks for a specific project
